# About

My name is Louis-Guillaume MORAND, a thirty-almost-forty yo developer who spends his time between night-writing code and drinking wine (vive la France !).

I spent the last ten years of my life as a consultant as Microsoft, mainly acting as a dev lead for large cloud projects and I quickly felt in love with DevOps, because on all projects I did, it was the missing part to make it efficient, wether it comes from the customer, the dev team, the pressure or anything else, everything DevOps could have been a solution to mitigate these issues.
